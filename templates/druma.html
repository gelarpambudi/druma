About Us

Druma is an Artifical Intelligence project made by students of Bandung Institute of Technology. Druma is made to detect trees that have the potential to harm the power line. Druma uses Deepforest library that has been trained with more than 20 thousand trees to detect trees, and hough transform algorithm to detect power lines. Druma can be used to predict your images from UAVs or satellite.
